🧠 NeuroVital – Smart Personal Health Monitoring System

📌 Overview

NeuroVital is a Python-based health monitoring system developed by Yasmin Firdhouse. The project was created with the goal of helping people monitor their daily health, understand common symptoms, and receive basic health guidance from home.

Many people cannot immediately visit a doctor or healthcare center for minor health concerns. NeuroVital provides an easy-to-use health assistant that encourages healthy habits and offers simple AI-based suggestions. It is not a replacement for professional medical advice, but it serves as a personal health companion for everyday wellness.

---

🎯 Project Objective

The primary objective of NeuroVital is to:

- Help users maintain healthy daily habits.
- Encourage preventive healthcare.
- Provide simple AI-based health suggestions for common symptoms.
- Allow users to monitor their overall health from home.
- Increase awareness of hydration, sleep, mood, and physical well-being.
- Promote healthier lifestyles through regular self-monitoring.

---

✨ Features

💧 Water Tracker

Tracks the number of glasses of water consumed daily and compares it with the recommended daily goal.

Benefits

- Encourages proper hydration.
- Helps prevent dehydration.
- Improves overall health awareness.

---

😴 Sleep Tracker

Records daily sleeping hours and evaluates sleep quality.

Benefits

- Promotes healthy sleeping habits.
- Alerts users about insufficient sleep.
- Encourages better daily routines.

---

😊 Mood Tracker

Allows users to record their daily mood.

Benefits

- Helps users become aware of emotional well-being.
- Encourages positive mental health.
- Provides basic motivation.

---

💊 Medicine Reminder

Stores medicine names and reminder times.

Benefits

- Helps users remember medications.
- Reduces the chance of missing doses.

---

🤒 Symptom Checker

Allows users to report common symptoms such as fever, headache, cold, and tiredness.

Benefits

- Provides simple AI suggestions.
- Encourages users to monitor their condition.
- Recommends consulting a doctor when necessary.

---

❤️ Heart Rate Monitor

Checks whether the user's heart rate falls within the normal range.

Benefits

- Detects unusually high or low heart rates.
- Promotes cardiovascular awareness.

---

⚖️ BMI Calculator

Calculates Body Mass Index using height and weight.

Benefits

- Determines weight category.
- Encourages healthy weight management.

---

🚨 Emergency Alert System

Detects critically low health scores and displays emergency warnings.

Benefits

- Alerts users during possible health emergencies.
- Encourages immediate medical attention when required.

---

📊 Health Dashboard

Displays all recorded health information in one place.

Includes

- Water intake
- Sleep hours
- Mood
- Heart rate
- BMI
- Medicine reminder
- Health score
- Risk level

---

📈 Health Risk System

Calculates the user's overall health risk level based on collected health information.

Risk Levels:

- Low
- Medium
- High

---

📝 Daily Health Report

Provides a summary of the user's overall health status.

---

💾 User History

Stores health records in a text file for future reference.

---

🤖 AI Health Tips

Provides personalized suggestions based on the user's health data.

Examples:

- Drink more water.
- Improve sleep schedule.
- Take proper rest.
- Maintain healthy habits.

---

💬 AI Health Chatbot

A simple chatbot capable of answering basic health-related questions.

Examples:

- Fever
- Cold
- Headache
- Stress
- Sleep
